The Deer Isle Volunteer Fire Department is made up of dedicated individuals who respond to fire and rescue calls in our community 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We are dispatched by the Hancock County Regional Communication Center to approximately 400 calls per year. Our volunteer firefighters attend regular training to keep their skills up-to-date.
